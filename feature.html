Login/Logout

This interface creates a restricted access to the web application. On providing the correct credentials, the home page is launched

Stock Level

This page appears by default on succesful login. It can equally be called upon by navigating through the elements and clicking the appropriate tab. As shown from the picture above, it depicts the amount of goods left after a successful sales or purchase transaction.

Sales and Purchase Reports

On every sales or purchase made, there is a corresponding PDF data created which can be seen by choosing range of dates.
